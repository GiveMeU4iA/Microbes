Are you paranoid yet?

You should be.
Antibiotic resistance is not your body resisting antibiotics due to a "tolerance".
Antibiotic resistance refers to bacteria becoming resistant to antibiotics through evolution, mutation, and horizonatl gene transfer.
Only 0.3% of all microbes have been discovered.
Between 2 and 10 million bacteria exist on your fingertips at any given moment.
Many vaccines become ineffective to antibiotics in about a year.
  • Methicillin: 1960 - 1961
  • Quinupristin/Dalfopristin: 1999 - 2000
  • Linezolid: 2000 - 2003
  • Daptomycin: 2003-2005
    • In 2019, 4.95 million deaths were associated with antibiotic resistance, 1.27m were a direct result of it.
    Overprescription of antibiotics has resulting in bacteria developing antibiotic resistance more rapidly over time.
    Antibiotic pollution has contributed to the problem by allowing bacteria to mutate and share genes (HGT) outside of a human host.
